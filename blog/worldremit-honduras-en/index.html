What is WorldRemit?

WorldRemit is an international money transfer company founded in 2010 in the UK. It operates in over 130 countries and lets you send money via app or website to bank accounts, cash pickup locations, and in some countries, mobile wallets.

For Honduras, their main service is direct bank deposits, though cash pickup is also available.

How Long Does the Money Take to Arrive?

For bank deposits in Honduras, WorldRemit generally delivers in minutes — most transfers arrive within 10 minutes if the transaction clears without additional review.

In some cases it can take a few hours if there's a security review. This is normal and applies to all services, not just WorldRemit.

Payment Methods Available

From the United States, you can pay with:

  • Debit card (recommended — no extra charges)
  • Bank account / ACH (no extra charges, may take slightly longer)

Avoid paying with a credit card unless it's urgent. The extra charge isn't worth it.

When to Choose WorldRemit Over Other Options

Choose WorldRemit if:

  • You want a reliable, consistent option without hunting for promotions
  • Your family has an account at one of the covered banks
  • You prefer a fixed, transparent fee ($3.99 always, no surprises)
  • You already have an account set up and the process is familiar

Consider Remitly if:

  • You're a new user and want to take advantage of a $0 first-transfer promo
  • You want to compare rates that day and see who's giving the most
  • You send very frequently and every lempira matters

Stick with Western Union if:

  • Your family doesn't have a bank account or needs cash in hand

How to Open a WorldRemit Account

The process is straightforward:

  1. Download the app (iOS or Android) or go to worldremit.com
  2. Create an account with your email
  3. Verify your identity (photo ID — automatic process, takes minutes)
  4. Add your payment method
  5. Enter your family member's bank details in Honduras
  6. Confirm the transfer

The first identity verification can take between 5 and 30 minutes. After that, transfers are immediate.

The Verdict

WorldRemit is an excellent option for sending money to Honduras — reliable, fast, and fairly priced. Remitly can beat it with promotions, but WorldRemit is extremely consistent. If you want a service you can depend on without comparing every time, WorldRemit delivers.

That said, rates change every day. Before you send, it's always worth a quick check to see who's offering the best rate on that specific day.
