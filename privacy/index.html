1. Introduction

Cassiopeia LLC operates tufaro.io under the Faro brand. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and the Faro WhatsApp bot.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the rate comparison service

4. Who We Share Your Information With

5. Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Some site features may be affected.

6. Your Rights

  • CCPA rights — California residents have the right to know what data we collect and to request its deletion
  • GDPR rights — EU residents have rights of access, rectification, erasure, and data portability

7. Data Retention

We retain your data while you use the Faro service, or for up to 2 years after your last interaction with our platform. After that period, we delete or anonymize your information.
