Last updated: May 2026 | Cassiopeia LLC

Faro earns a small commission when you choose a provider through our links. This does not affect the price you pay or the order in which results appear.

1. What Is an Affiliate Program?

When you click a link on Faro and sign up or make a transfer with a remittance provider, Faro may receive financial compensation from that provider. This is our primary business model — it allows us to keep the service completely free for you.

This practice is standard on comparison websites and is regulated by the U.S. Federal Trade Commission (FTC), which requires its transparent disclosure.

2. This Does NOT Affect Your Experience

  • The price you pay is the same whether you use our affiliate link or not
  • Providers are ranked by best rate, not by the commission they pay to Faro
  • We never recommend an inferior service just because it pays a higher commission
  • You can go directly to the provider's website and get the same price

3. Objectivity of Our Comparisons

Our rate comparisons are based on publicly available data obtained directly from provider websites. Rankings and results exclusively reflect who offers the best received amount for the specific destination and amount being sent.

The integrity of our comparisons is fundamental to our mission. If we lost your trust by showing biased results, we would cease to be useful — and that would destroy our business.
